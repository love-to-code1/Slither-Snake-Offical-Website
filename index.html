Slither

Slither Snake, the AWSOME game above is created by The PROgrammer.
Inspired by Google's original game: Snake, it is a fun and easy-to-play game!
The simple controlls are:

• Up arrow or W to go up

• Down arrow or S to go down

• Left arrow or A to go left

• Right arrow or D to go right

• avoid the worlds edges

• avoid your snakes body

• eat the apples
